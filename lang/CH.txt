Mdima wamdima?
Yambani maseŵera a Goose:
Wosewera amaseŵera nthawi yake poponya mawiri awiri. Malingana ndi chiwerengero kapena chiwerengero chomwe amachipeza, wosewera mpira amasewera bokosi lake pa bokosi.
Pali malamulo a masewera a Goose kulemekeza molingana ndi chiwerengero chimene munthu amapanga kapena bokosi limene likugwa.
Tiyeni tiwone bwinobwino malamulo awa:
✓ Kumayambiriro kwa masewerawo, ngati mmodzi mwa osewerawo atapitirira 9 ndi 6, ayenera kupititsa patsogolo pake pomwepo mpaka chiwerengero cha 26. Ngati apanga 9 ndi 4, adzapita ku nambala 53.
✓ Ngati pa masewerawo, wosewerawo akugwera pa tsekwe, amapitanso patsogolo chiwerengero cha mfundozo.
✓ Ngati wosewera mpira akudutsa 6, ayenera kupita kumalo 12.
✓ Wochita maseŵera amene amagwera pa bokosi 19 lofanana ndi hotelo ayenera kudutsa nthawi yake nthawi yachiwiri.
✓ Wochita maseŵera amene amagwera pa bokosi 31 lofanana ndi chitsime akuyembekezera wina wosewera mpira kuti afike nambala yomweyo ndi kutenga malo ake.
☼ Aliyense amene agwera pa bokosi 42 lofanana ndi labyrinth adzabweranso ku bokosi 30.
☼ Amene amapita mkati 52 akufanana ndi ndende akuyembekezera wina wosewera kuti abwere nambala yomweyo kuti achoke.
✓ Wochita maseŵera omwe amapita ku bokosi 58 akugwirizana ndi bokosi la Death's Head ayambanso masewerawo kuyambira pachiyambi
☼ Aliyense amene akuphatikizidwa ndi osewera wina pa bokosi lomwelo adzayenera kupita ku bokosi pomwe wosewera mpira uja asanathe kusewera.
Mmene mungapezere masewera a Goose:
Kuti mupambane masewera a Goose, muyenera kukhala woyamba kufika pa bokosi lomaliza 63 koma muli ndi udindo wofika pabokosili.
Ngati wochita maseŵerayo akupanga mapikidwe apamwamba kuposa chiwerengero cha mipata yolekanitsa iye ku chigonjetso, adzayenera kubwereranso ndi mabokosi ena ochuluka.
