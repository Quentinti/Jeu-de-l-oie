Qala umdlalo we-Goose:
Umdlali ngamunye udlala ijika lakhe ngokuphonsa amadayisi amabili. Kuye ngenombolo noma inombolo etholakalayo, umdlali uqhubekela phambili ebhokisini lakhe lepayipi ngebhokisi.
Kukhona imithetho yomdlalo we-Goose ukuhlonipha ngokusho kwenombolo umuntu akwenzayo noma ibhokisi eliwela ngalo.
Ake sihlolisise le mithetho:
☼ Ekuqaleni komdlalo, uma omunye wabadlali eba ngu-9 ngo-6 no-3, kumele aqhubekele phambili isikhonkwane sakhe kuze kube nombolo 26. Uma enza 9 ngo-4 no-5, uzoya kwinombolo 53.
☼ Uma phakathi nomdlalo, umdlali wehla ku-goose, uphinde athuthukise inani lamaphoyinti akwenzile.
☼ Uma umdlali edonsa u-6, kumele ahambe esikwelethini 12.
☼ Umdlali owela ebhokisini 19 elihambelana nehhotela kufanele adlulise isikhathi sakhe ngesikhathi sokuguquka kwe-2.
☼ Umdlali owela ebhokisini 31 elihambisana nomthombo uzolindela omunye umdlali ukuba afike enombolweni efanayo futhi athathe indawo yakhe.
☼ Noma ubani owela ebhokisini 42 elihambisana ne-labyrinth uyobuyela ebhokisini 30.
☼ Ngubani ongena ku-52 afanelana nejele uzolindela omunye umdlali ukuba eze enombolweni efanayo ukuze ashiye.
☼ Umdlali oya ebhokisini 58 ehambisana nebhokisi I-Death's Head izoqala umdlalo futhi kusukela ekuqaleni
☼ Noma ngubani ojoyina omunye umdlali ebhokisini elifanayo kuzodingeka aye ebhokisini lapho omunye umdlali ngaphambi kokudlala.
Indlela yokuwina umdlalo we-Goose:
Ukuwina umdlalo we-Goose, kufanele ube ngowokuqala ukufika ebhokisini lokugcina 63 kepha unesibopho sokufika ngqo kuleli bhokisi.
Uma kwenzeka umdlali enza amaphuzu edayisi aphezulu kunani lezikhala ezihlukanisa naye ekunqobeni, kuzodingeka abuyele emuva ngamabhokisi amaningi.
Igeyimu eyenziwe ngu:
